Photography

Photography to me is a form of art. It is away to capture a story. I really enjoy photography, It has become a new hobby of mine. There is so much to yet learn as I am still only starting off. I am a very creative person. Photography fulfills my creativeness. I enjoy making beautiful things, and sharing my creativeness with others hoping they get the same satisfaction as I do.Photography also gives me a reason to travel and explore the world. I get to see the world in a way I never really seen it before. I get the pleasure of memorable experiences when they travel somewhere new or go on holiday. I also get to put my creativity, and my new found love for photography, into action.
